About Me

Hello there! I'm an Applied Accounting undergraduate at SAB Campus in Sri Lanka, affiliated with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Sri Lanka (CA Sri Lanka). I'm currently working as a Junior Staff Assistant at Sampath Bank Super Branch in Negombo.

My journey into accounting began with a fascination for numbers and financial analysis. I was drawn to how accounting serves as the backbone of business decision-making and financial transparency. This interest has evolved into a commitment to master the intricacies of financial reporting and contribute to organizational success through accurate financial management.

Beyond academics and work, I'm an avid movie enthusiast and novel reader. I find joy in immersing myself in different stories and narratives. I love listening to pop music, which helps me stay focused during long hours of study and work. These interests help maintain a healthy work-life balance and often provide fresh perspectives in my professional approach.
